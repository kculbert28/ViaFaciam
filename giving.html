Giving

Invest in pathways for women.

Every gift helps the Via Faciam Foundation support women’s initiatives, mentorship, professional access, emergency support, community partnerships, and long-term programming.

What your gift supports

Giving to the Via Faciam Foundation sustains mission-focused work: practical support, mentorship, campus programming, professional resources, and partnerships that expand opportunity for women at Harvard and beyond.

Impact areas

Six core areas of support

Women’s Initiatives

Programs that raise awareness, create connection, and expand access to resources.

Mentorship

Career guidance, intergenerational connection, and leadership development.

Professional Access

Workwear, workshops, networking, and career-readiness resources.

Emergency Support

Flexible support for urgent needs that can otherwise block opportunity.

Community Partnerships

Collaboration with organizations serving women and families.

Long-Term Programming

Sustainable infrastructure for recurring initiatives and durable impact.

Current priority

Support professional clothing access for single mothers

This year’s giving priority helps fund clothing drives, community partnerships, and practical resources that support single mothers preparing for interviews, work, and professional growth.
