Mission first

Expanding opportunity through programming, partnerships, and community investment

The Via Faciam Foundation is the nonprofit philanthropic arm historically connected to Seneca, with an independent mission focused on expanding opportunity for women through programming, mentorship, practical support, and long-term community partnerships.

Our work is grounded in the belief that women thrive when they have access to supportive networks, leadership opportunities, professional resources, and communities that invest in their futures.

What guides our work

Opportunity. Leadership. Community. Dignity.

Opportunity

Opening doors to resources, relationships, and experiences that help women move forward.

Leadership

Supporting women as organizers, mentors, advocates, and community builders.

Community

Creating spaces where women can connect across backgrounds, classes, and generations.

Dignity

Centering practical support that helps women feel prepared, respected, and confident.

Current Initiative

Professional clothing support for single mothers

This year’s initiative focuses on helping single mothers access professional and everyday clothing for interviews, work, and career-building moments. The goal is practical, personal, and rooted in dignity: helping women step into opportunity feeling prepared and supported.

Origin story

Connected to the Seneca, centered on public impact

The Via Faciam Foundation grew from a community of Seneca members and alumnae who wanted to turn relationship, leadership, and service into sustained philanthropic impact. Seneca remains part of the foundation’s history; the foundation’s operating identity is its mission to create pathways for women.

Inspired by Aut viam inveniam aut faciam — “I shall either find a way or make one” — The Via Faciam Foundation exists to create pathways where none yet exist.
