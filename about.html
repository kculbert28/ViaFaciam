Mission

Creating pathways where none yet exist.

The Via Faciam Foundation advances opportunity, mentorship, leadership, and practical support for women at Harvard and beyond.

Our purpose

The Via Faciam Foundation invests in programs and partnerships that help women access community, professional resources, leadership development, and support systems that can endure long after a single event or moment of need.

We focus on work that is tangible and relationship-driven: mentorship, campus programming, professional access, community partnerships, and practical support for women navigating barriers to opportunity.

Guiding values

What guides our work

Opportunity

We expand access to resources, networks, and experiences that help women move forward.

Leadership

We support women as organizers, mentors, advocates, and community builders.

Community

We create spaces where women can build meaningful relationships and mutual support.

Dignity

We prioritize practical support that honors women’s confidence, agency, and ambition.

Our connection to The Seneca, Inc

The Via Faciam Foundation was founded by members and alumnae of the Seneca and remains inspired by that origin story. Today, the foundation is presented first through its nonprofit mission: expanding opportunity for women through programming, partnerships, and community investment.
