Partners

A network of partners, mentors, and alumnae supporters.

The Via Faciam Foundation is strengthened by the people and organizations who share its commitment to women’s opportunity, mentorship, and community support.

Partnership makes the mission durable

Our work depends on alumnae, mentors, community organizations, donors, and campus partners who help translate good intentions into sustained programming and practical support.

Partners may support mentorship, professional access, event collaboration, giving campaigns, resource drives, or long-term initiative planning.
